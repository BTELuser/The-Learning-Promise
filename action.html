Take Action for Education

Ways You Can Help

Share articles, social media posts, or organize discussions at school or community groups to educate others about the challenges children face globally.

Donate to organizations like UNICEF, Room to Read, or local education charities that provide schools, materials, and teacher training to underserved communities.

Offer your time to tutor students locally or online, helping them improve literacy, numeracy, or other essential skills.

Collect gently used books, stationery, or educational tools to send to schools and libraries in low-resource areas.

Support initiatives and campaigns that aim to reduce gender and socioeconomic disparities in schooling, ensuring all children have equal opportunities.

Read reports, watch documentaries, and follow educational NGOs to stay informed about the state of global education and where you can help most effectively.
