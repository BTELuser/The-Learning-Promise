The Global Education Problem

Across the world, millions of children are unable to attend school or receive a quality education. Poverty, lack of resources, and conflict prevent many children from accessing learning opportunities.

In some regions, schools are too far away or lack trained teachers and learning materials. Families living in poverty may depend on children to work instead of attending school.

Sub-Saharan Africa faces some of the greatest education challenges. Without access to education, many children have fewer opportunities for employment and a better future.
